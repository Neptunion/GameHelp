How to play


Use the arrow keys or WASD to move around the level. Use shift or Z to roll, left click to shoot and reload, R to reload, Shoot the enemies 3 times to kill them. To damage bosses you need to hit them from behind.

The tank will turn to face you whenever you touch the ground, jump over it and shoot it before you land to damage it.


The bull will toss you around and kill you quickly, don't let it catch you.


   

These powerful weapons are hidden, you can find them and pick them up.
